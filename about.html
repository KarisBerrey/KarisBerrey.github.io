About Karis

My name is Karis. I am a reader, writer, athlete, and adventurer who aspires to write truth, goodness, and beauty, push back against the darkness of this world and reflect elements of The Greatest Story Ever Told, the story of the Bible. In my writing—whether fiction or non-fiction—I desire to glorify God and destroy my own tendency to drift towards complacency.

Join me as, by God's Grace, I continue to trudge down this road of continuous growth!
